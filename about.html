My Story

Hi, thanks for stopping by to check out my personal website!

My name is Sergey, I'm a UX/UI designer from Munich.

Electronics engineer by education, I have always endeavoured to develop systems to the maximum of the capabilities provided by the target system, to make the user's work as efficient as possible. My experience allows me to combine abilities at the intersection of different disciplines, being "Designer for programmers" and "Programmer for designers".

I love solving complex problems and know how to keep simple ones uncomplicated.
